Highway Traffic Game

About the Game

Highway Traffic is an exciting racing game where you navigate through busy roads, avoiding cars and obstacles while collecting points.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move your car. The longer you survive and the more cars you overtake, the higher your score.

  • Avoid colliding with other vehicles.
  • Use speed boosts strategically.
  • Try to beat your high score!

Game Features

Feature Description
Realistic Traffic Experience real road-like traffic patterns.
Multiple Vehicles Choose from different cars to race with.
