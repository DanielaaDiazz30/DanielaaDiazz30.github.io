Highway Traffic Game

About the Game

Highway Traffic is designed to challenge users' mulitasking skills by testing their speed and reflex skills. In this high intensity game, players are challenged to race against the clock while also literally racing through a highway of moving traffic. Players are to navigate through traffic while dodging other vehicles on the road and striving to achieve high speed scores for points. The key is users must avoid collisions through the chaotic lane weaving.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move your car. The longer you survive and the more cars you overtake, the higher your score.

  • Avoid colliding with other vehicles.
  • Use speed boosts strategically.
  • Try to beat your high score!
